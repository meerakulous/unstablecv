Cross-validation for Longitudinal Datasets with Unstable Correlations
This demo is based on the KDD 2025 paper "Cross-Validation for Longitudinal Datasets with Unstable Correlations". It simulates an outcome using two features: one consistently predictive over time (the stable feature) and another occasionally predictive (the unstable feature). Different cross-validation strategies—random CV, block CV, and our proposed method—are compared. Random and block CV may underestimate the unstable model’s error and favor it, while our method avoids this pitfall and provides more reliable model selection.
